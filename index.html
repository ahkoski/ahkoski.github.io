About Me

Amanda Koski

I am a dynamic Learning Designer and User Experience Expert dedicated to enhancing the corporate learning experience through innovative instructional design, engaging training sessions, and customized learning programs.

With a passion for gamification, innovation, and learner-centric design, I have successfully implemented impactful training programs across various industries, including education, financial technology, and law. I currently lead the development of strategic learning materials and user-centric solutions, driving significant improvements in user engagement and technology adoption.

My philosophy is that learning should be accessible, engaging, and applicable beyond the classroom. I strive to create on-demand and just-in-time learning solutions that support continuous learning and professional growth.

I bring a passion for gamification, innovation, and strong user-empathy to every project I am a part of. I thrive when I am learning new skills and trying new things. My goal is to continue to remain flexible and ever changing in my approach.
