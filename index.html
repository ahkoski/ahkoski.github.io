About Me

Amanda Koski

I am a skilled professional working to improve the corporate learning process through creative instructional design, engaging and fun training sessions, and thoughtful tailor-made training programs.

I believe that all learning should be relevant and engaging and shouldn’t be reserved to the classroom alone. I want to work with organizations to make on-demand and just-in-time learning options available for employees to use as on the job aids and to foster an environment of continuous learning.

I bring a passion for gamification, innovation, and participant-centered learning to every project I am a part of. I thrive when I am learning new skills and trying new things. My goal is to continue to remain flexible and ever changing in my approach to learning and development.
